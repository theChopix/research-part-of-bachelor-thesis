Sankey Diagram Visualization

A Sankey diagram is a type of flow diagram that displays the movement of objects or substances through a system, using the width of the arrows to represent their flow quantity. They are commonly used to visualize energy, material or financial flows, and are useful for identifying patterns and inefficiencies in a system. With a proper customization we can use plotly Sankey Diagrams for visualization of decision trees and use their flow structure to imitate flows of data through the model.


Plotly

Plotly is a Python library used for creating interactive data visualizations. It provides a wide range of chart types and customization options to help users explore and communicate insights from their data. Among them is also the creation of a Sankey diagrams.


In the research part, we used a sankey diagram to visualize the flow of training data.


This otherwise more descriptive method is, with the help of the plotly library, used more sophisticatedly in the main part of the work - in the application itself.
